Global Coffee Trade

Exporters

Having been well-established as a commodity as early as

the 1500s, coffee has since spread across the globe

far from its Ethiopian roots.

Coffee trees grow best a mild climate: between 21°C and 29°C.

The "Bean Belt" around the Equator is perfect for this.

Countries can usually be grouped into primarily exporters or importers.

Importers

Countries outside of the Coffee Belt must rely primarily on importing coffee beans from the exporters countries.

The United States and many European countries are among the top importers.

Coffee Flavour

The Coffee Quality Institute regularly review coffee beans around the world.

Each batch is judged based on different quality measures, standardised out of 10.

Specialty coffee is grouped by two main species: Arabica and Robusta.
